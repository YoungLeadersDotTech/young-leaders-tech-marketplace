# Contributing and support

## Status

Personal, experimental projects shared in the open. Things may change or break without notice.

## Using these

Free to use, fork, and adapt under the MIT License (see LICENSE). Attribution is appreciated but
not required. No warranty, and use at your own risk, especially for the action-taking agents.

## Contributing

Issues and pull requests are welcome. Small, focused PRs are easiest to review. By contributing
you agree your contribution is licensed under the same MIT License.

## Support

This is a side project maintained in spare time, so support is best-effort with no guaranteed
response time or roadmap. If something is broken, an issue with steps to reproduce is the most
useful thing you can send.
